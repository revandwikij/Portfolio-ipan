Lumen Branding

A complete visual identity for Lumen, a boutique lighting design studio — built around warmth, clarity, and a sense of quiet confidence. From logo system to social templates, every piece was designed to feel cohesive and unmistakably "Lumen."

My role on this project.

I led the visual identity from concept to delivery, working closely with the Lumen team to translate their brand values into a flexible design system.

01

Brand strategy & visual direction — defining tone, mood, and core identity principles.

02

Logo design & logo system — primary mark, monogram, and usage guidelines.

03

Color palette & typography pairing for print and digital applications.

Social media templates, posters, and promotional assets for launch.
