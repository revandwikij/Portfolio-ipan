About Me

The person behind the pixels.

Hi, I'm Ivan — a 26-year-old UI/UX designer who believes good design should feel invisible.

For the past six years I've worked with founders, agencies, and product teams to design interfaces that are simple, accessible, and genuinely enjoyable to use. I care about the details most people never notice — spacing, rhythm, the way a button feels when you press it.

When I'm not designing, I'm exploring new tools, experimenting with generative AI workflows, and refining my craft one pixel at a time.
